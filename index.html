Real-Time Tracking

Our advanced GPS technology provides precise location data for all public transport vehicles in real-time.

1
GPS devices installed on buses transmit location data every 30 seconds
2
AI algorithms process this data to predict accurate arrival times
3
Users see live bus locations and ETAs on interactive maps

SMS Access

We ensure everyone can access transport information, regardless of smartphone ownership or internet connectivity.

1
User texts the bus stop code to our dedicated number
2
System processes the request and fetches real-time data
3
Instant reply with next bus arrivals, routes, and timings

Safety First

Our comprehensive safety features ensure peace of mind for all passengers, especially women and vulnerable groups.

1
One-tap SOS button immediately alerts authorities and emergency contacts
2
Live journey sharing lets family track the passenger's route in real-time
3
Safe route recommendations avoid high-risk areas, especially at night

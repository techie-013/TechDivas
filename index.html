SMS Access

Access bus information without a smartphone or internet connection

Text Message

To: 773-77-BUSHI

Message: BUS HISAR12

Reply: Bus #HIS102 (Route 12A) arriving in 8 mins. Next bus: 15 mins.

How it works:

  1. Text "BUS [stop code]" to 773-77-BUSHI
  2. Receive real-time arrival information
  3. Get route details and next bus timings

Popular Stop Codes:

  • HISAR12 - Central Station
  • HISAR23 - City Mall
  • HISAR07 - University Gate
  • HISAR45 - Medical College
